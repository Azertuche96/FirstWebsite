Who is Seventeen?

Seventeen is an all male kpop group. Group is comprised of thirteen members. The Group preforms as the whole thirteen but they also preform in three different subunits.

Seventeen Subunits

Preformance Unit: Hoshi, Jun, The8 and Dino. Hip-Hop Unit: S.Coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu and Vernon. Vocal Unit:Woozi, Jeonghan, Joshua, DK, Seungkwan
